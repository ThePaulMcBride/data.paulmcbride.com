The little town I now live it is a western rodeo town and they use the word bucked very differently than would be common in Northern Ireland. Having the sense of humour a 15 year old boy this provides me with no end of laughter. The town slogan is "Let 'er buck" and this morning I came across this sign and it cracked me up.
