The #fediverse has a real potential to democratise access to information. The status quo is an algorithm that we have no insight over showing us content that will keep us hooked.
With Mastodon we see things posted by the people we follow in the order they posted them. Frankly it’s a little boring and that’s a good thing. Mastodon is most engaging when your involved in conversation with other people.
On Twitter you could doomscroll forever. Here, you have conversations, then get on with your life
